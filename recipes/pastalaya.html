Pastalaya

Description

This is an amazing recipe from youtuber Cajun Ninja. It's a great twist on the classic Cajun dish Jambalaya using Bowtie pasta instead of rice. This recipe will feed an army and be a smash at any family gathering!

Ingredients

  • 1 lb pork
  • 1 lb boneless chicken thighs
  • 1 lb smokes sausage
  • 1 onion
  • 1 bell pepper
  • 1 bunch of green onion
  • 2 sticks of celery
  • 1 tbsp minced garlic
  • 3 beef bouillon cubes
  • 1/4 cup vegetable oil
  • 3 1/2 cups watter
  • 1 can cream of chicken soup
  • 1 lb bow tie (or favorite) pasta
  • Salt and Pepper
  • 1/2 tbsp of cajun seasoning
  • 1 tbsp hot sauce
  • 2 tbsp Kitchen Bouquet (or other browning sauce)

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 300 °F
  2. Heat a pot over medium heat
  3. Chop pork, chicken, and sausage
  4. Chop onion, bell pepper, green onion, and celery, then move to fridge
  5. Season pork well with salt and pepper
  6. Add vegetable oil to pot, then start searing the pork
  7. When you no longer see pink, turn the heat to low and simmer for 25 minutes, stirring occasionally
  8. After 25 minutes, raise heat back up to medium, add the chicken, let cook for 10 minutes, stirring occasionally
  9. After 10 minutes, remove pork and chicken with a slotted spoon into a bowl
  10. Add the sausage to the pot, cooking 10 minutes or until you see some browning
  11. After 10 minutes, remove the sausage with a slotted spoon into the same bowl
  12. Add the onion, bell pepper, green onion, celery, garlic, cajun seasoning, and beef cubes
  13. Saut√© until completely soft, roughly 30 minutes
  14. Add the water, cream of chicken, hot sauce, and kitchen bouquet. Raise the heat
  15. When the soup has blended, add the pasta and meat back in. Mix well
  16. Once everything is mixed nicely, press down on the top so that all pasta is covered by liquid
  17. Bring the mix to a boil. Once boiling, cover, turn the fire off, and put the pot in the oven
  18. Cook in oven for 1 hour
  19. After 1 hour, remove the pot, then set aside for 5 minutes before uncovering
  20. After 5 minutes, mix well and serve
